Welcome

If you've made it here, welcome!


This is the start of my newest adventure. Along with going to school full-time to become certified in my dream job, I am taking on the task of documenting my journey, from accomplishments to failures. The point of this is to *hopefully* inspire others to pursue their dreams, and as always to make the world a little more accessible for everyone. I want to normalize the process and show that no matter your talents, there will be struggles, but you can overcome them. No one promised that this life would be easy, no one promised us everything, but if you shoot for the moon, at least you'll land among the stars.
