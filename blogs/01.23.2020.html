We've Reached a Milestone!!!

I had my first loss of information! I am still not quite sure what happened but I ended up losing all of my website information. Shocking I know but I was mad for all of about 2.5 seconds before I recognized this for the opportunity it was. It gave me a chance to redo some information and really look at how I was building this site, spoiler alert: I was doing it the difficult way. Let me explain.

When you make a webpage, each part of the information goes into things called elements, and you can name these elements. My idea of organizing was naming each individual element like it was going out of style, so while I was rebuilding I took the opportunity to really look at the elements I was using and try to figure out how I could streamline the process. I write all of this in html and the issue there is that I format everything as I go. Now I don't have to. I've streamlined the process to ease my workload and make it easier to do what I am thoroughly enjoying doing.

This also gave me a chance to add a "beautification" process and add some elements to the background and each element that I feel gave the website a little more depth. You are more than welcome to check out the source code by right clicking the webpage and clicking "View page source" to bring it up in another window. If you're just learning to code it can be very informative to check out the pages you enjoy and really just delving into the coding of each page. Soon I will do a walkthrough of how I built this website so stay tuned for that information if that's something that interests you. For now, I am going to sign off. It's been a long, but enjoyable day.
